Causeway Point is located in the heart of Woodlands Regional Centre, next to the Woodlands MRT Station and bus interchange. With more than 250 shops, it is the largest mall managed by Frasers Property Retail.

Anchor tenants are Bagus, Cathay Cineplexes, Courts, Food Republic, Kiddy Palace, Metro, POPULAR Bookstore, Timezone and Uniqlo.

In 2011, Causeway Point won a Green Mark Platinum Award by Building & Construction Authority. It was voted Best Suburban Mall by The Straits Times and awarded the Best A&P Effort Award by the Singapore Retailers Association in 2004. In 2015, it was also awarded the BCA Universal Design Mark Gold Plus award.

Parents rooms are located at every level (next to the toilets).

Shoppers can enjoy free Wi-Fi access with Wireless SG.
